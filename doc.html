Essential Documents Guide

Navigating travel documentation can be complex. This guide outlines the basic documents required for common travel-related processes. Please note that requirements can vary by country and individual circumstances.

For International Travel

  • Passport: Must be valid for at least 6 months beyond your planned date of return.
  • Visa: A valid visa for your destination country, if required. Check the specific embassy requirements.
  • Air Tickets: Confirmed return or onward journey tickets.
  • Proof of Identity: Another form of government-issued ID like an Aadhar or PAN card can be useful.

For a New Indian Passport

  • Proof of Present Address: Aadhar Card, Voter ID, recent utility bill, or bank passbook.
  • Proof of Date of Birth (DOB): Birth Certificate, School Leaving Certificate, or Aadhar Card.
  • Photographs: Recent passport-sized photographs with a white background.
  • Annexures: Specific declaration forms as required by the Passport Seva Kendra.

For a Tourist Visa (General)

  • Visa Application Form: Completed and signed form for the specific country.
  • Passport & Photos: Original passport and recent photos as per the embassy's specifications.
  • Travel Itinerary: Day-by-day plan of your trip, including flight and hotel bookings.
  • Proof of Financial Means: Recent bank statements or ITR to show you can fund your trip.
  • Proof of Employment/Leave Letter: A letter from your employer stating your role and approved leave.
